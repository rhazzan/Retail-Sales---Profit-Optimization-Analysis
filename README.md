# Retail Sales & Profit Optimization Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data from a global electronics company to identify key revenue drivers, evaluate business performance, and uncover areas for profit optimization.

The analysis was conducted using Excel and Power BI, with a focus on transforming raw data into actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Retail businesses often struggle to:
- Track true profitability across products and regions
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Identify why sales performance fluctuates over time
- Retain customers and maximize lifetime value

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
- Power BI (Data Modeling & Dashboard Development)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 💰 Financial Performance
- Total Revenue: 878.03K  
- Total Profit: 514.05K  
- Profit Margin: ~58% (high profitability)

However:
- Revenue and profit declined by ~44% year-over-year
- Strong short-term growth observed month-on-month (~300%)

---

### 🛒 Sales Channel Analysis
- In-store sales dominate (75.9%)
- Online sales contribute only 24.1%

👉 Indicates underutilization of digital sales channels

---

### 📦 Product & Category Performance
- Computers generate the highest cost and profit
- Other strong categories: Cameras, Cell Phones, TV & Video
- Business is heavily dependent on tech-related products

---

### 🎨 Customer Preferences
- Top-performing product colors:
  - Black (8.13M)
  - White (7.02M)
  - Silver (6.10M)

👉 Neutral colors drive the majority of sales

---

### 🏷️ Brand Performance
Top-performing brands:
- Adventure Works
- Contoso
- Wide World Importers

---

### 👥 Customer Behavior
- 56.22% of customers did not return (low retention)
- Only 43.78% are repeat customers

👉 Indicates a major opportunity to improve customer loyalty

---

### 🌍 Regional Insights
Top-performing locations:
- Utrecht
- Washington
- Texas

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

- Improve customer retention through loyalty programs and targeted marketing
- Expand online sales channels to reduce dependence on physical stores
- Focus on high-performing product categories (Computers, Cameras)
- Optimize inventory by reducing low-performing product lines
- Leverage customer preference for neutral-colored products in marketing and stocking

---

## 💼 Business Value
This analysis helps businesses:
- Identify profit-driving products
- Reduce revenue leakage
- Improve customer retention
- Make data-driven strategic decisions
